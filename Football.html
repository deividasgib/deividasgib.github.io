Football In My Life

I started playing football at a very young age, in my hometown Silute, which is a city not too far from where I study in Klaipeda. I played for different age groups there essentially being promoted to men's first team when I entered 1th grade in high school. I found some life long friends there and it shaped me as a person that I am today.

After graduating from high school, I understood that it will be difficult to get a good quality of life from football there, so I decided to apply for LCC International University. During my first weeks there, I learned that they also have a football team here. So, I definitely wanted to be a part of the team. I went to trials, workouts with the team and eventually got accepted to a team.

So, I was part of LCC Moose for four years, and learned so much from this experience. I improved my skills, strength as well as game knowledge. These goals that I scored in front of LCC community was really special, and will remember it for a long future. I feel blessed to have my number 10 retired and feel grateful for everyone involved there.

If you would like to work on your game with me, or apply to LCC Moose men's team, deifnitely go ahead and contact me here.
